About Me

Introduction

Hey! I'm Jess and I am a first year University of Waterloo student studying Computer Science.
I was born in Kingston, ON, and later moved to Brockville, ON. I love being active, mostly aquatic sports as growing up on the St.Lawrence means you cannot miss out on the joys of boating, swimming and tubing. That being said, I was apart of the Upper Canada River Otters Swim Club based in Brockville, where I consistently competed at the national standard.
Nonetheless, I treat my academics the same way I found success in swimming. I'm diligent and always trying to focus on the process. One of the most important lessons I got from swimming was that having a strong mindset is key. Hence, as a computer science student with no prior experience, I refuse to let that define myself and my oppurtunities. I am eager to learn and dedicated to finding my spot in the world of computer science.
